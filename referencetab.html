These are the different types of notes that you will see on a sheet of music. A whole note has one beat per measure. On the left side of a bar of music it may have numbers like 4 over 4 or may just have the letter C. C means common time meanin there are 4 beats per measure. Think of the song twinkle twinkle little stars, this song will have 4 beats/measure. Jin-gle bells-jin-gle-bells, each section is a beat. The other types of notes are just 2x faster or even 16x faster. That is what the missing thing is at the end of the picture.

A rest is very similar to notes themselves. As it's called, it is a rest in your music playing where you completely rid of all sound for a small amount of time.The different types of rests last for as long as one note of its name. A whole rest lasts as long as a whole note , a quarter rest lasts as long as a quarter note and so on.
